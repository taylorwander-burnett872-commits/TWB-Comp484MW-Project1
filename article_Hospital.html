Community Spotlight: Umbrella’s Partnership with Raccoon General Hospital


Umbrella stands as the number 1 worldwide pharmaceutical researcher and producer. While an impressive feat on its own, we here at Umbrella feel that doesn't mean much if these state of the art treatments and medicines aren't making their way into the hands of those that need them in a timely and efficient manner. That is why Umbrella is excited to showcase our recent partnership with Raccoon General Hospital. Through this partnerhsip, Umbrella hopes to deliver fast and effective brand new pharmaceuticals to those most in need of them.
